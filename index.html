Welcome to my home page.
I am an Assistant Professor at School of Artificial Intelligence, Sun Yat-sen University.
My research interests are in:

  • Computer Vision (e.g., Action Analysis and Person Re-Identification)
  • Machine Learning (e.g., Temporal Information Prcessing and Multi-View Problems)

I was a postdoc researcher of Simon Fraser University and work with Professor Greg Mori. I got my Ph.D. degree from Queen Mary University of London. My advisors were Professor Tao Xiang and Timothy Hospedales. My Master advisor was Professor Wei-Shi Zheng at Sun Yat-sen University.
